The thirsty crow

Once upon a time,there was a crow. One day while it was flying in the hot desert, the crow got thirsty.

the crow stated flying over houses in search of water but unfortunately, there were no houses in the desert.

except one house. near it was a pot of water. when the crow saw that, it was very happy.

so right then it flew over to the pot of water and reached for it. but the water was too low

for the crow.

Then the crow had a plan! so it started to take and drop rocks into the pot.

little by little, because of the crow's hard work, the water started rising! after sometime.

finally the crow got water to drink!

After drinking the water, the crow flew away and lived happily ever after.

Moral of the story:Where there is a will there is a way.
