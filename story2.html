The fox ad the grapes

Once upon a time in a far far land, there was a fox.

one day when the fox was roaming in the forest, it saw a bunch of grapes!

so right away, the fox jumped to the grapes in a tree branch.

but the branch was to high and then he also missed the grapes.

so then he tried again and again but he missed.

what kept him going was the thought that these might be the best grapes in the world.

so then he walked of a short distance. then he ran and leapt forwad.

but alas! he could not reach it.

he kept triyng and trying. but he could not reach it.

so then the fox finally gave up. he said: what a fool am I!

wearing myself out for a bunch of grapes! these could not even be the best grapes.

this could be so sour grapes maybe. Away he walked very scornfully.

Moral of the story:

many people despise what is beyond their reach.
